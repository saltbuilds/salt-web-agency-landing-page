## Image Specifications

- **Format:** PNG with transparent background recommended
- **Size:** Square format (recommend 512x512px or larger for quality)
- **Style:** Should match the style of salt-police.png (cartoon/mascot style)
- **Character:** All should feature the salt shaker character in different roles/scenarios
- **Colors:** Can use the brand colors (Navy: #000000, Aqua: #81C7D4) for consistency

## Notes

- The images will be displayed at 96x128px (mobile to desktop) within circular containers
- Each will have a subtle colored background from the design system
- Images should be centered and work well when scaled
- Keep the character recognizable as a salt shaker across all variations
